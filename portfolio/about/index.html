About Me

I am 19 years old and I have been studying journalism since my junior year in high school. I fell in love with sports early on at a young age so it was a very easy career choice for me. Throughout my time in high school, I was a producer, camera man, anchor, reporter, and even a technical director. I got familiar with the TD board, different sony cameras, and the sensation of being on-air.

Throughout my two years at Syracuse, I have been involved with WAER Radio and Z89 Radio. It is here where I grew my passion to talk live on the air along with being able to enhance my creativity. I am also a member of CitrusTV where I have been out on the field and have had numerous opportunities to grow my on-air skills. Finally, I have interned with the club-basketball team which gave me a sense of professionalism within the field of broadcasting.
